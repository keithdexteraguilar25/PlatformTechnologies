Capstone Project

EVENTO

A Web-Based Event Evaluation System for Supreme Student Council at Batangas State University - The National Engineering University

Abstract

With the usual way of the end-users, the developers have been given the idea of making the process into an automated and more practical type of system. The evaluation system is a system designed to do the usual evaluation tasks they do, but in an automated manner. The Supreme Student Council will be able to handle their usual job with more ease and in a more common manner. And with the help of the system (with an internet connection), they can do their jobs anytime, anywhere.

In Batangas State University, Supreme Student Council are used to do the evaluation using the manual or the usual way. They encode each detail of the students and their project manually on a spreadsheet and give out the ratings and remarks on a piece of paper.

Objectives

  • Objective 1

    To provide availability of information about services related to student organizations and a client-server based automated event evaluation system.

  • Objective 2

    To develop a web-based application that will allow the Supreme Student Council - Alangilan and advisers to evaluate the events of student organizations and their projects through an innovative way of assessment.

  • Objective 3

    To streamline the recording process for student organizations and reduce discrepancies in terms of Pablo Borbon GAWAD Awards.
